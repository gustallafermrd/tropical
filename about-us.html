THE SOUL OF
GLENCOE

A Young Vision with
Mexican Soul.

Tropical was born from the dream of a young Latino couple who wanted to bring a piece of their heritage to Glencoe, Minnesota. What started as a shared passion for authentic Mexican flavors quickly evolved into a modern cafeteria that bridges tradition with a futuristic aesthetic.

In our vibrant Glencoe cafe, we've created a space where the warmth of Mexican hospitality meets a sleek, modern energy. Every drink we serve and every sweet treat we craft is a testament to our roots, reimagined for the modern palate.
